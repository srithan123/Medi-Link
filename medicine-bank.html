Community Medicine Bank

About the Medicine Bank

Our community medicine bank allows people to donate unused medicines and request medicines they need. This helps reduce waste and ensures everyone has access to essential medications.

Important Notice

Only unexpired, properly stored medicines in their original packaging can be donated. Controlled substances and injectable medicines cannot be accepted.
