Our NGO Partners

We collaborate with reputable NGOs to extend healthcare services to underserved communities. These partnerships help us reach more people and provide essential medical care.

Provides free medical camps in rural areas and urban slums across India. Specializes in maternal and child health services.

Urban Health Initiative

Focuses on healthcare for homeless populations and migrant workers in cities. Provides mobile clinics and emergency medical services.

Connects volunteer doctors with remote villages lacking medical facilities. Provides telemedicine services and training for local health workers.
